Welcome to Military TAPS

By a Vet. For Vets.

This site was created while I was in one of the many TAPS classes occuring around the country. The one issue some of us saw in the class, was that there was not a "one stop shop" for the retiring or separating member, to find all the links to the loads of useful information. So, this is an attempt to get as much information onto one site for any member of any military component to use to their advantage.
Below are the three main sites you, as the member, will want to sign up for BEFORE you retire. DS-Logon will no longer be supported by the VA as they're switching to LOGIN.GOV.
TAPS Master Schedule

All TAPS hosted classes throughout the United States and beyond. This site is for the member to sign up and begin the transition.

Visit Website
Login.gov

The VA uses Login.gov as their preferred method. You could use DS Logon but that will be no longer supported soon. So, set your login.gov account BEFORE you retire.

Veterans Administration

After you setup your Login.gov, go to the VA site to set up your premium VA account.
