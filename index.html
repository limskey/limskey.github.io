Military TAPS

This site was created while I was in one of the many TAPS classes occuring around the country. The one issue some of the members saw in the class, was that there was not a one stop shop for the retiring or seperating member to find all the links to the loads of useful information.

So, this is an attempt to get as much information onto one site for any member of any military component to use to their advantage.
